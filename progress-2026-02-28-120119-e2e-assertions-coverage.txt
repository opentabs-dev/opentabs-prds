## Codebase Patterns
- node_modules may or may not be pre-installed; verify with `ls node_modules/.bin/tsc` before assuming build readiness
- E2E tests in `e2e/` follow: setupToolTest → page.evaluate to trigger action → waitFor polling → assertion
- The pre-commit hook runs lint-staged (prettier + eslint fix) on staged `.ts`/`.tsx`/`.json` files automatically
- `result.content` in MCP tool call results is an array; `.toContain()` and `.toMatch()` work on the stringified form (Vitest matches on the array itself)
- When no browser extension is connected (npm-discovery test), the error is "extension not connected. please ensure the opentabs chrome extension is running." — not "closed" or "no matching tab"
- `onNavigate` E2E tests must clear `__opentabs_onNavigate_urls` before triggering navigation (setupToolTest may pre-populate it)
