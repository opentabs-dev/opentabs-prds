- `waitForAuth()` is exported from `carta-api.ts` alongside `isAuthenticated()`. It polls via `waitUntil()` with 500ms interval and 5s timeout, resolving `true` on success and `false` on timeout.

- The `isReady() returned false` warning from `opentabs-plugin build` is expected when running in a non-browser environment (no cookies present) — it is a warning, not an error.
