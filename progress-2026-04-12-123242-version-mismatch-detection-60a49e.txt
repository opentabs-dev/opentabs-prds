## Codebase Patterns
- CLI commands are in platform/cli/src/commands/; shared helpers go in platform/cli/src/ (not in commands/)
- CLI entry point is src/cli.ts â†’ src/commands/index.js; new shared modules must be imported transitively from there to avoid knip "unused file" errors, OR added as knip entry points
- import.meta.resolve('@pkg/package.json') pattern is used for resolving nested package.json paths (ESM project, not require.resolve)
- Monorepo fallback path for mcp-server from cli/src/: join(cliDir, '..', '..', '..', 'mcp-server', 'package.json')
- setup.ts has a local getCliVersion() that will be refactored to import from version-info.ts in US-003
