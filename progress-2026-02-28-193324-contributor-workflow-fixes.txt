## Codebase Patterns
- `scripts/clean.ts` handles two phases: Phase 1 (always) removes dist/ and .tsbuildinfo; Phase 2 (--all) removes node_modules and standalone projects
- Plugin directories in `plugins/` are standalone (not in npm workspaces); their tsbuildinfo files are separate from platform/* ones
- tsc composite projects with .tsbuildinfo can skip emitting dist/ if the build info says "up-to-date" even when dist/ is absent
