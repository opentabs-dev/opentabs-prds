## Codebase Patterns
- Prettier enforces a specific Tailwind class ordering: `pt-` before `pr-` before `pb-` before `pl-` (clockwise). When splitting `px-N` into `pl-N pr-M`, write them as `pr-M ... pl-N` to satisfy the formatter.
- Shadow sizes in `styles.css`: `shadow-md` = 4px right offset, `shadow` = 3px, `shadow-sm` = 2px. Compensate with extra right padding/margin equal to the shadow offset.
