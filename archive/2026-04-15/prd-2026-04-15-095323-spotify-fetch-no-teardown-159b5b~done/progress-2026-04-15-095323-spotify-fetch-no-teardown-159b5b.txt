## Codebase Patterns
- Plugins implement `override teardown(): void` to clean up on adapter deactivation (see aws-console, e2e-test for examples)
- The plugin-sdk defines `teardown?(): void` as an optional method on OpenTabsPlugin (plugin-sdk/src/index.ts:125)
- Plugins that patch globals (window.fetch, DOM observers) should export a reset/disconnect function from their API module and call it from teardown()
