## Codebase Patterns
- `getCliVersion()` uses `join(cliDir, '..', 'package.json')` — compiled output in `dist/`, so `..` resolves to the package root. Works identically in global npm installs.
- `getMcpServerVersion()` uses `import.meta.resolve('@opentabs-dev/mcp-server/package.json')` as primary, with monorepo fallback `join(cliDir, '..', '..', 'mcp-server', 'package.json')` (from `platform/cli/dist/`, two levels up reaches `platform/`).
- To expose `package.json` via `import.meta.resolve`, add `"./package.json": "./package.json"` to the `exports` field in `package.json`.
