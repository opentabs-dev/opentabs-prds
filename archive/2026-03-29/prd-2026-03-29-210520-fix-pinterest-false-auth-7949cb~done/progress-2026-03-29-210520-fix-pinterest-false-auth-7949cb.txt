## 2026-03-30 - US-001
- Fixed `isAuthenticated` to also check for `_pinterest_sess` cookie alongside `csrftoken`
- The `csrftoken` cookie alone was insufficient because Pinterest sets it for all visitors (logged in and anonymous)

- Files changed: `plugins/pinterest/src/pinterest-api.ts`
- **Learnings for future iterations:**
  - Pinterest's `csrftoken` is not a reliable auth signal — it exists for CSRF protection on all pages
  - `_pinterest_sess` is the session cookie that indicates a logged-in user
  - Plugin worktrees may not have `node_modules` for standalone plugins; run `npm install` if build fails with SDK resolution errors
