## Codebase Patterns
- The Select primitive (Select.tsx) is the reference design for dropdown styling. Menu.tsx should match its Content and Item classes.
- Retro primitives support variant props (e.g., `variant="destructive"` on Menu.Item) to avoid raw className overrides in consumers.
- Menu.Item keeps `gap-2` and `[&_svg]` helpers that Select.Item doesn't have, because menu items have icons alongside text.
- Only three files consume Menu.Item: PluginMenu.tsx, BrowserToolsMenu.tsx, and Menu.stories.tsx.
