## Codebase Patterns
- `readConfigPort()` in platform/cli/src/parse-port.ts caches the config.json port for the process lifetime — calling it after writing a new port to config.json returns the NEW port, not the old one
- `resolvePort(options)` priority: --port flag → OPENTABS_PORT env → config.json → DEFAULT_PORT (9515)
