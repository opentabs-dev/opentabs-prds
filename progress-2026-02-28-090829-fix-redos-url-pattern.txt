- **Learnings for future iterations:**
  - The `[-.]?` → `[-.]` change is semantically equivalent for valid hostnames: valid domain labels are always separated by `.` or `-`, so removing the optionality only affects invalid sequences of pure alphanumeric chars without separators (which are just a single label, handled by the outer `[a-z0-9]+`)
